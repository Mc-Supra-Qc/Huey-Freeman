The Freeman Family

The Boondocks tells the story of two African-American brothers, Huey and Riley Freeman, and their grandfather Robert Freeman. Become rich, Robert Freeman decides to leave Chicago for an imaginary town in the southern United States called Woodcrest. So Huey and Riley, living with their grandfather, leave the Chicago ghetto to find themselves in a predominantly white, wealthy and peaceful suburb.
